About

We are committed to honoring traditional techniques combined with the finest ingredients San Luis Obispo County has to offer. We offer a seasonal menu consisting of traditional regional ramen styles informed by the offerings of our local farmers and ranchers. We also offer an ever changing selection of robata-yaki and small plate izakaya dishes to compliment our craft cocktails, beer, wine and sake selections. Tyler’s Ramen Shop is dedicated to providing a shared community space that brings together all walks of life under the umbrella of good food, good drink and joyous atmosphere. We take pride in ensuring that everyone who comes to Tyler’s Ramen Shop has the best experience possible.
